About NYSEC CTF

NYSEC CTF is an annual Capture The Flag (CTF) competition organized by the New York Security Enthusiasts Club. Our mission is to promote cybersecurity education and provide a platform for enthusiasts to hone their skills through hands-on challenges.

Our CTF features a wide range of challenges covering various domains including web security, cryptography, reverse engineering, and more. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced hacker, there's something for everyone at NYSEC CTF!
